FAQ

  • Q: Something is not working.
    A: Open the launcher, click on three dots on top right side and select "Reset configuration files".
  • Q: Something is still not working.
    A: It might be an issue with this port, describe the problem in detail and email omw@xyz.is. Attach textual output of "adb logcat" if possible.
  • Q: How can I use a controller/gamepad?
    A: All gamepads should be supported natively. Just connect it to your Android device normally, and it should work fine. You can remap the buttons from the setting menu in game.
  • Q: How do I use the mouse?
    A: You can use the touchscreen to control the mouse just by clicking anywhere you want. You can also use the right stick for an alternative laptop-like mouse cursor control (for example, if an on-screen button overlaps whatever you want to click).
  • Q: How do I configure the controls?
    A: Select "On-screen controls..." in the launcher.
  • Q: How do I install mods?
    A: Drop the mods to your data files folder and activate them from the "Mods..." menu. More info here.
  • Q: Water looks weird.
    A: In the "Advanced" section change "Graphics library" to "GLESv2".
  • Q: The framerate is too low.
    A: In the "Advanced" section set "Physics FPS" to a lower value and enable the "Force simple collision box" option. You can also try switching between GLESv1 and GLESv2 graphics library.
  • Q: It's crashing all the time, what can I do?
    A: Try switching between GLESv1 and GLESv2 "Graphics library" setting.
  • Q: The menus/text are too small, I can't see anything!
    A: In the "User interface" section change "GUI scaling factor" to 2 or a higher value.
